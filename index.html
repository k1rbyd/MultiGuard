Abstract

MultiGuard is a unified explainable framework that detects and explains forgery across both visual and textual domains. Combining rationale-augmented text reasoning with attention-based visual forensics, it bridges modalities through a Cross-Modal Reasoning Bridge (CMRB) to produce coherent and interpretable outputs. The system achieves 94.7% accuracy and an F1-score of 0.93 on diverse datasets, setting a new benchmark for multimodal misinformation detection.
